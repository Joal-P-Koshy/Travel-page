ALIMATHAA

Counted among the loveliest islands in the Maldives, Alimathaa is known for exciting tourists with its splendid greenery and turquoise waters.

BAROS

Life at Baros has always been a fulfilment of harmony. Collection of handcrafted beachfront and overwater villas are built from timber and sandstone.

MIRIHI

Surrounded by the endless azure of the Indian Ocean, an eco-friendly place. Mirihi is one of the smallest resorts in Maldives.

GULHI BEACH

The most beautiful beach in the Maldives. Fine sand, crystal clear sea that changes color during the day in all shades of blue and green. Not a lot of coral.

VAADHOO

Vaadhoo Island is well connected to the rest of the Maldives. It is located around eight kilometers away from Male, which is the capital city of the Maldives.
